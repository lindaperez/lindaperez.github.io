    About

    I'm an M.S. Data Science student at Northeastern University (Silicon Valley campus), with a B.S. in Computer Science and several years of software and data engineering experience earlier in my career. My foundation spans full-stack web development, databases, and ETL — which now anchors my work in analytics, machine learning, and applied AI.

    I began with software developer internships at Banco Agrícola and Sudeban, building full-stack applications with Symfony, Liferay, PHP, and SQL. As a Database Specialist at Universal Bank B.O.D I built 100+ SQL Server pipelines, scheduled jobs, and Cognos reports used by business managers for decision-making. At VASS LATAM I designed PL/SQL and Oracle scripts powering 80+ web services and built ETL pipelines that cut query times and accelerated reporting, and as a freelance data scraper for Neuvoo/Talent.com I maintained 20+ daily production scraping scripts serving millions of users.

    I later took a planned career break for full-time parenting while continuing professional development through UC Berkeley Extension, DeepLearning.AI / Stanford, LinkedIn Learning, Coursera, and self-directed projects — then entered the M.S. in Data Science program to go deeper into machine learning, NLP, data mining, and the full data science lifecycle: cleaning, preparation, training, testing, deployment, evaluation, and maintenance.

    Today my focus is connecting my software engineering and data background with applied AI — using SQL, Python, machine learning, NLP, RAG systems, and cloud analytics to produce deeper analysis, build faster models, and deliver higher-quality, more accountable solutions. I'm looking to grow in a data science or machine-learning role where I can combine software implementation, data engineering experience, and modern AI to build practical, reliable systems.
